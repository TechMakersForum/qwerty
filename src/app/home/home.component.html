About

Varnam caterers is name famous for providing the best outdoor catering services in Trivandrum and through out kerala. Varnam Catering Services welcomes the opportunity to serve you. We have exotic and scrumptious menus, which delights the mood of the food lovers. For creating a lip-smacking feast, guests can choose the appetizers, entrees and desserts.
